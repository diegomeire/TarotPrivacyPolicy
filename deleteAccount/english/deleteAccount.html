How to delete your account


1. Open the app

2. If you're not logged in automatically, tap on the user icon to login


3. Do the Log in


4. To remove your account, go to the user section by tapping the user icon on the main screen



5. Go to "Remove User"


6. Confirm the following messages

ATTENTION! THIS CAN'T BE UNDONE AND ALL OF THE DATA WILL BE PERMANENTLY REMOVED
